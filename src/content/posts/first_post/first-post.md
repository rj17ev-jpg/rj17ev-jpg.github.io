I am so happy to be finally doing this. 
<br />

For the first time in my life, I'm experiencing a strong need to *create* things. This is the place where I attempt to actually *do something* with all my ideas and thoughts and scattered pieces of writing over the years. This instinct is so insistent now that it feels physically impossible to live in the 'lurking' way anymore. I want to be out there in the world, I want to engage with things and with people. 
<br />

This is also a way for me to deal with certain parts of myself I've been ignoring for a long time. It has always been very difficult to finish projects and be consistent with anything, and the resulting guilt and shame and avoidance have built up until they've literally ruined my life. This is the opportunity I'm giving myself to overcome all of that and start off with a clean slate. Maybe I'll learn a lesson or two in this process.
<br />

I've also very recently had the realization - thanks to other writers and creators ! - that it's far more wonderful to *make* new things using and applying what you learn, and it's also the best *way* to learn. 
<br />

I also want to blog about what's going on in my life - the projects I'm working on (for some accountability to actually finish them !), and things I'm reading, learning, or exploring. This is also very new for me - I want to share my life with others. It certainly promises to be more fulfilling than hiding every single thing about myself. 
<br />

Anyway - welcome !! I love you.
