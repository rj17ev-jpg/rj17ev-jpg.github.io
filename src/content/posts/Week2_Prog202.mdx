## What this is

This assignment is for PROG-202, Human Experimental Workshop. This semester we're focusing on analyzing cardiac data and relating it with neuroscience. 

The participants performed two tasks. The first was a 'count' task with two conditions: [slow, fast] in which individual letters appeared on the screen and participants recorded their heartbeats while watching the screen. 

In the second task, participants viewed two videos [rest, move] and recorded their heartbeats.

These are the main goals of this assignment:
* Exclude noisy data
* Apply a Gaussian filter to the data 
* Compute power spectra for each condition
* Create visualization of the data
* Perform a one-sided, paired t-test to compare the means of the BPMs between the two conditions of each experiment
* Perform a nonparametric bootstrap test to compare the mean BPMs in two condition in each experiment.
